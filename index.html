City destroyer

How to play:

There are four monsters below. By clicking on a monster, you will add a specific amount of destruction points to your total destruction score. You destroy the city, and win the game, by matching your total destruction score to the given number below. You lose the game if your total score goes above the given number below. The destructive power (represented through numbered score) of each monster is hidden from you until you click on it.Each time when the game starts, the game will change the power of each monster.
